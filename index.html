Why we're meeting

This offsite aligns Sales, Customer Success, Growth, Marketing, and Strategic Accounts on a unified sales and adoption strategy for Forma Design — specifically the Site Design and Building Design bundles.

The Gap

Today, there's no shared model for how these functions work together. Each is building its own approach — but nobody has agreed who owns what, where the handovers are, or what the user should actually experience at each stage.

This Group is a Strategy Unit

Multiple teams, one strategy. This workshop surfaces the realities, clarifies roles, identifies key initiatives, and produces an aligned one-page strategy document.
